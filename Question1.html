Question 1. How many moles are in 10 mg of cetirizine HCl?


The process:

I had such a tough time finding questions to answer. As said before, I really wanted to go down to the atomic relationships of allergens, but they would not be so easy to quanitfy. I therefore thought this question was a good first one. Sourcing PubChem, a credible source and the nation's library of medicine, the molecular weight of cetirizine HCl is 425.35 grams per mole (g/mol). From there I used this conversion factor:

1 mol C21H26Cl2N2O3⁄425.35 g
