The active ingredient in his Aller-tec tablets is cetirizine hydrochloride (C21H26Cl2N2O3), and the bottle already tells me that in one tablet there are 10 milligrams of this chemical. Initially I thought I could go as far as to find the chemical reactions that occur between cetirizine HCl and a certain amount of a specific allergen, however the factors that compose an allergen are much too complex and time consuming for a project that I read too much into. Therefore I set on a new course to find the conversion factors for milligrams, grams, and kilograms, as well as mass ot mole conversions.
